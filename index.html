Tower
Bells

Welcome to the world of tower bells - large bells of the size and kind that are usually hung in towers, often in combinations that form various kinds of musical instruments. Almost always made of cast bronze, weighing from several pounds to several tons, they produce the original "heavy metal music," with a wordless voice that resonates in the heart of mankind.
